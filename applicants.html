Applicants

Applicants & Prospective Students

Deciding on business schools can be daunting. The Kellogg Veterans Association (KVA) is here to help. If you're considering Kellogg, please reach out to us. We can introduce you to other veterans, give you balanced perspectives toward Kellogg, help you prepare your application, and arrange for you to meet vets and attend the classes.

points of contact

We encourage you to contact a KVA recruiter from your respective service or for the program in which you are interested. We have recruiters for US military services, International, Israeli, and Asian veterans. This person will be your best source for relevant admissions information and assistance with applications.

What can we do for you?

Our slogan is "Vets First." This means two things to us. First and foremost, your success as a vet is our TOP PRIORITYâ€”regardless of the business school or career path you choose. We strive to give you fair and balanced advice and are available to help you decide if and where you should go to business school. "Vets First" also means that we offer considerable assistance with your application. So engage us for:

  • Advice about business school and Kellogg. Please contact us no matter your stage in the application process. Dozens of vets contact us every week (US military & international) so don't hesitate to engage us early and often.
  • Hosting you while you visit. We organize a Military Preview Day in the April and October, which give you the most bang-for-your-buck while visiting (Next Preview Day | Visiting Kellogg). However, we also meet visiting vets on a weekly and "stop-in" basis. You can join us for class, lunch, or just coffee or beer. Additionally, our "Sofas-for-Soldiers" program offers a free place to stay while you visit. Click the link to learn more.
  • Assistance. We advise vets on application essay topics, and read and edit essays and resumes for vets. So talk to us when considered essay topics, and email essays and resumes to us when you are done. We'll give feedback to strengthen your application
  • Interview Preparation. Before you interview, please contact us. We want to ensure you are prepared and confident going into your interview. Vets generally do well, but understanding the nuances of Kellogg's culture is important prior to speaking with admissions or alumni
    • Overall, we want you to succeed no matter your future path. So engage us early and often, and we are eager to show you what makes Kellogg the RIGHT school (most important) for many vets. Read below for more information before you apply.
